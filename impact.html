The Impact of Pollution on Pollinators

Pollution significantly impacts pollinators by disrupting their natural habitats, food sources, and reproduction cycles.

  • Respiratory Problems: Air pollution affects pollinators’ ability to breathe, reducing their health and lifespan.
  • Water Contamination: Pollutants in water sources are harmful to pollinators that drink from them.
  • Pesticide Exposure: Pesticides weaken pollinators’ immune systems, making them vulnerable to disease and death.
  • Loss of Habitat: Land pollution reduces plant diversity, depriving pollinators of food and shelter.
