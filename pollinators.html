Pollinators at Risk

Pollinators such as bees, butterflies, and bats are essential for biodiversity and food production. However, pollution and habitat loss are threatening these vital species.

Bees

Bees are among the most important pollinators. Pollution, pesticides, and habitat loss are causing significant declines in their populations worldwide.

Butterflies

Butterflies, sensitive to environmental changes, are increasingly affected by pollution and climate change, threatening their habitats and food sources.

Bats

Bats are nocturnal pollinators that face threats from light pollution and habitat destruction, which disturb their feeding and breeding habits.
